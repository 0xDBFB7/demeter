4.5. The Console tab¶

Any messages from the ATOMS and FEFF runs are displayed in this windows, including error messages. If you suspect something has gone wrong in your FEFF calculation, check this tab. If the problem is one that is recognized by ARTEMIS, you will get a helpful diagnostic suggestion.
