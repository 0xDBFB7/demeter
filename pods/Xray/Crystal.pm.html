NAME

Xray::Crystal - A crystallography wrapper

VERSION

This documentation refers to Demeter version 0.9.26.

SYNOPSIS

This imports Xray::Crystal::SpaceGroup, Xray::Crystal::Cell and Xray::Crystal::Site into your program. It also imports strict and warnings into your program.

  use Xray::Crystal;
  my $sg   = Xray::Crystal::SpaceGroup->new;
  my $cell = Xray::Crystal::Cell->new;
  my $site = Xray::Crystal::Site->new;
